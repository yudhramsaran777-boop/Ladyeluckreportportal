👤 Character Design Sheets

  • Build Slightly pudgy, round head, huge expressive eyes, rubbery limbs
  • Color Blue shirt, red tie, tan skin (#FBBF24), dark brown hair
  • Expression Perpetually stunned — jaw drops, stars orbit head, sweat drops
  • Animation Stretchy physics — squash & stretch on every impact
  • Voice Nasally everyman, exasperated, think Charlie Brown grown up

🎬 Scene-by-Scene Storyboard

SCENE 1 · "THE MORNING FROM HELL" 0:00 – 0:08

The Morning From Hell

Joe wakes up, steps out of bed onto a banana peel, slides across the floor and face-plants into the dog's water bowl. Stars orbit his head. The dog walks away unimpressed.

"...Good morning to me."
WIDE SHOT → CLOSE BOING! SPLAT! Slide Whistle Slapstick Comedy
SCENE 2 · "THE COMMUTE" 0:08 – 0:16

The Commute

Joe steps outside. Sunny everywhere except directly above him. The instant he lowers his umbrella — downpour soaks only him. A smug cartoon duck steals his umbrella. Rainbow appears in the dry zone next to him.

"Why does this always happen to ME?!"
MEDIUM SHOT Thunder Crack · Sad Trombone Ironic / Pathetic
SCENE 3 · "THE SCRATCH TICKET" 0:16 – 0:24

The Scratch Ticket

Joe at the diner. He scratches a ticket — two 7s revealed. Last number: a cartoon face that sticks its tongue out at him. Ticket literally mocks him. Joe's jaw bounces off the table.

"The ticket just MOCKED me. THE TICKET. MOCKED. ME."
CLOSE-UP on ticket → Pull back Raspberry SFX · Spring Boing Peak Absurdity
SCENE 4 · "ENTER LADY E LUCK" 0:24 – 0:40

Enter Lady E Luck

GOLDEN EXPLOSION. Lady E Luck descends from above in a shower of coins and playing cards. She surveys the damage, pushes her sunglasses down, and holds up her glowing phone showing LadyELuck.com. The banana peel, duck, and mocking ticket all hang their heads in shame.

"Honey. You've been out here risking it all... on a scratch ticket?"
LOW ANGLE → EPIC WIDE Gold Fanfare · Coin Rain Goddess Entrance Energy
SCENE 5 · "THE GLOW-UP" 0:40 – 0:52

The Glow-Up

Joe opens LadyELuck.com on his phone and logs in as VIP. Instantly: the sun blazes, his hair is perfect, coins rain down, the dog comes back wagging, and the waitress pours the coffee flawlessly. Joe leans back like royalty. Lady E Luck gives a chef's kiss.

"Okay. OKAY. I get it now."
WIDE → SMASH ZOOM IN on phone Triumphant Cartoon Fanfare Victory / Transformation
SCENE 6 · "THE TAG" 0:52 – 1:00

The Tag / Logo Card

Lady E Luck floats back into golden clouds, winks at camera, and snaps her fingers. The Lady E Luck logo BURSTS onto screen surrounded by sparkles and coin rain. Joe pops in the corner with his thumbs up.

V.O.: "Lady E Luck. VIP gaming, daily bonuses, and the kind of luck you actually EARN."
Joe: "Don't be me. Be a VIP."
SMASH CUT · LOGO PUSH IN FINAL BOING · Logo Sting Triumphant / Call to Action
